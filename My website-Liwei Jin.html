Food Sharing

One of my favorite dishes from my hometown is Fried meat with pickled vegetables. Here's a simple recipe:

  1. Add oil to the pan. When the oil is hot, add shredded ginger, dried chili and peppercorns and stir-fry until the aroma overflows; then add the tenderloin and stir-fry until 70% cooked.
  2. Pour an appropriate amount of vegetable oil into the pan again. When the oil is hot, add the pickled vegetables and stir-fry for a while, then pour the stir-fried tenderloin into the pan and continue to stir-fry evenly.
  3. Before serving, add scallion segments, light soy sauce and an appropriate amount of salt (the pickled vegetables themselves are salty, it is best to taste them before deciding whether to add salt) and stir-fry evenly!
